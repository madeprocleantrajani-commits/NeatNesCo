## PRODUCT TESTING FRAMEWORK

### The 15-Product System
| Tier | Count | Purpose | Ad Budget |
|------|-------|---------|-----------|
| HERO | 3 | Viral potential, highest priority | $10-15/day each |
| TEST | 6 | Ad testing candidates | $5-10/day each |
| BACKUP | 6 | Reserve, rotate in when others fail | $0 until promoted |

### Product Acceptance Filter (Automated)
A product enters the catalog ONLY if it passes ALL:
- [ ] TikTok viral signals (500k+ views on related content)
- [ ] Amazon rating > 4.3 stars
- [ ] 2.5x+ markup possible (sell >= 2.5x supplier+shipping)
- [ ] NOT a brand product (no trademark risk)
- [ ] Price sweet spot: $10-60 (impulse buy range)

### Win/Kill Rules
- **WINNER:** ROAS > 2.0 after 7 days, scale budget 2x
- **MAYBE:** ROAS 1.0-2.0, optimize creative/targeting for 7 more days
- **KILL:** ROAS < 1.0 after 7 days, replace with BACKUP product
- **MEGA WINNER:** ROAS > 3.0, scale aggressively to $50-100/day

## AD STRATEGY

### Phase 3 Initial Setup ($30-45/day)
- **Platform:** Meta (Facebook + Instagram)
- **Campaign Structure:**
  - Campaign 1: HERO Product 1 - CBO $15/day
    - Ad Set 1: Broad targeting (18-44, interest-based)
    - Ad Set 2: Lookalike 1% (when data available)
  - Campaign 2: HERO Product 2 - CBO $15/day
  - Campaign 3: HERO Product 3 - CBO $15/day
- **Creative Types:**
  - UGC-style video (problem -> solution)
  - Before/after carousel
  - Lifestyle image + strong copy

### Scaling Rules
- ROAS > 2.0 for 3 days -> increase budget 20%
- ROAS > 3.0 for 3 days -> increase budget 50%
- ROAS < 1.0 for 3 days -> kill ad set
- Never increase budget more than 50% in one day
